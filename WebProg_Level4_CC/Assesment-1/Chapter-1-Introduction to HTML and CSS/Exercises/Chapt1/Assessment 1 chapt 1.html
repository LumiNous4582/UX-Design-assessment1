Hawaiian Pizza Recipe

The classic Hawaiian pizza that combines a sweet & savory flavors that has that crispy crust topped with tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, pineapple chunks, and ham.
Whether if its your fan favorite or not, try it for the first time and you may like it

Ingredients

  • 1/2 recipe pizza dough)

  • 1 Tablespoon (15ml) olive oil

  • 1 Tablespoon (8g) cornmeal

  • 1/2 cup (127g) pizza sauce (homemade or store-bought)

  • 1 and 1/2 cups (6oz or 168g) shredded mozzarella cheese

  • 1/2 cup (75g) cooked ham or Canadian bacon, sliced or chopped

  • 1/2 cup (82g) pineapple chunks (canned or fresh)

  • 3 slices bacon, cooked and crumbled

How To Make

  1. Preheat Oven to 220C(425F)

  2. Prepare the dough, Lightly flour a surface and roll out 1/2 recipe pizza dough into a 12-inch circle, then sprinkle 1 Tablespoon (8g) cornmeal on a baking sheet or pizza stone to prevent sticking, transfer the pizza dough onto the prepared baking sheet.

  3. Add the sauce, spread 1/2 cup (127g) of pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving it about 1/2 inch around the edges for the crust

  4. Drizzle 1 tablespoon (15ml) of olive oil over the pizza for a golden and crispy crust

  5. Baking the pizza, place the pizza in the preheated oven and bake it for 12-15 minutes until the crust is brown and the cheese bubbly

  6. Slice and serve, remove the pizza from the oven, slice 6-8 pieces and enjoy your hawaiian pizza
