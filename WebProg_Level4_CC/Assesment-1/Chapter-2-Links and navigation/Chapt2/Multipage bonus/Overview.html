Overview

Blue Archive (ブルーアーカイブ) is a mobile gacha RPG developed by NEXON Games and published by Yostar. The game was first released in Japan on February 4, 2021.

A global version launched on November 8, 2021, published by NEXON, with support for Korean, Traditional Chinese, and Thai.

A Chinese version followed on August 3, 2023, published by Yostar, featuring support for Simplified Chinese.

The game blends strategic real-time combat with visual novel-style storytelling, immersing players in the mysterious city-academy of Kivotos, where they take on the role of Sensei-- guiding students, solving incidents, and uncovering hidden secrets.
